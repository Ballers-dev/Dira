About Daira

Public Health Policy and Practice

Our Foundation

Daira, Public Health Policy and Practice was established as a Canadian registered public health consultancy to bridge the gap between evidence-based research and practical policy implementation. We specialize in translating complex public health data into actionable strategies that improve population health outcomes.

Our consultancy operates at the intersection of policy analysis, health systems evaluation, and strategic communication, providing comprehensive solutions to organizations seeking to enhance their public health impact.

Leadership Profile

Dr. Anis Kazi

MBBS, MPH, IPFPH, FRSPH

Dr. Anis Kazi brings extensive expertise in public health policy and practice, with a distinguished background in primary health care transformation, universal health coverage, and health systems strengthening. His comprehensive experience spans multiple sectors including public health agencies, development organizations, and international health systems.

With advanced training in medicine and public health, Dr. Kazi combines clinical understanding with policy expertise to deliver evidence-based solutions that address complex health challenges at local, national, and international levels.

His approach emphasizes rigorous analysis, stakeholder engagement, and sustainable implementation strategies that ensure lasting impact in public health policy and practice.
