About Daira Public Health Consulting

Canada's Leading Public Health Policy and Systems Strengthening Consultancy

Our Mission

Daira, Public Health Policy and Practice is a Canadian registered consulting and advisory service dedicated to advancing equitable, resilient, and sustainable health systems globally. The firm serves governments, development agencies, and multilateral partners through expert-led technical assistance and strategic guidance.

Positioned as an institutional leader in health systems transformation, Daira combines multidisciplinary expertise with global reach to deliver evidence-based solutions that strengthen health systems and improve population health outcomes worldwide.

Our Values

Evidence-Driven

All recommendations and strategies are grounded in rigorous research, data analysis, and proven best practices in public health.

Partnership-Oriented

Collaborative approaches that engage stakeholders, build local capacity, and ensure sustainable implementation of health solutions.

Results-Focused

Commitment to measurable outcomes, continuous improvement, and lasting impact on population health and health system performance.

Our Approach

Daira delivers comprehensive technical assistance through multidisciplinary teams with recognized global expertise in health systems transformation.
