Expertise

Multidisciplinary and globally recognized approach to health systems transformation

Daira's multidisciplinary expertise spans the full spectrum of health systems strengthening, from policy design to implementation support. The consultancy's globally recognized approach combines technical excellence with practical experience across diverse health system contexts and challenges worldwide.

Health Policy Analysis and Design

Comprehensive policy development, analysis, and reform support across health system governance, financing, and service delivery frameworks.

Health Systems Assessment and Strengthening

Strategic evaluation and strengthening of health system building blocks, including service delivery, workforce, information systems, and supply chains.

Public Health Security and Emergency Preparedness

Health security planning, emergency preparedness, outbreak response, and resilience building for health systems facing complex emergencies and health threats.

Primary Health Care (PHC) and Universal Health Coverage (UHC) Reform

Technical assistance for PHC transformation and UHC implementation, including integrated care models, service delivery optimization, and health financing reforms.

Strategic Communications, Advocacy, and Stakeholder Partnerships

Evidence-based communications strategies, stakeholder engagement planning, and advocacy support for health system reforms and policy implementation.

Capacity Building, Training, and Knowledge Management

Organizational development, technical training programs, knowledge transfer, and institutional strengthening for sustainable health system improvements.

Multi-sectoral Collaboration (including linkages with SDGs)

Cross-sectoral partnership development, SDG alignment strategies, and integrated approaches to health that address social determinants and promote health equity.
